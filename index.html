Privacy Policy for The Baking Pro

Last updated: 26 January 2026

The Baking Pro (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use The Baking Pro mobile application (“the App”).

1. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you create an account or log in to the App, we may collect your name, email address, and login credentials.

b) Contacts Information

If you choose to use the Import from Contacts feature, the App may access and collect full names, phone numbers, email addresses, and physical addresses (if available).

Contacts are accessed only when you explicitly initiate the import action.

c) Business & App Data

You may also provide data related to customers, orders, deliveries, recipes, and inventory.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide core app functionality, manage customer contacts, support deliveries, and improve app performance.

3. Data Storage and Processing

Data is securely stored on our backend infrastructure (currently using Supabase). Service providers may change, but all providers are required to meet appropriate security standards.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with trusted service providers or when required by law.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Data is retained only as long as necessary. Users may delete data within the App or request deletion via email.

6. Permissions

The App requests Contacts permission only to allow users to import contact information into the App.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Baking Pro is not intended for children under the age of 13.

8. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
