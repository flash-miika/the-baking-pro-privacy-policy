Account Deletion – The Baking Pro

The Baking Pro allows users to request deletion of their account and associated personal data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

  • Open the app and navigate to Settings → Delete Account
  • OR email support@thebakingpro.com from your registered email address with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Account profile
  • Email address
  • Business details
  • User-generated content
  • Usage data linked to your account

Data That May Be Retained

  • Anonymized analytics data
  • Records required for legal compliance (if applicable)

Processing Time

Deletion requests are processed within 7 days of verification.

If you have any questions, contact us at support@thebakingpro.com.
